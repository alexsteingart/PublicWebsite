Marketplace as a Service

Providing peer-to-peer marketplaces that look, feel, and act like your brand, for your products, without the hassle of building and running it.

Expanding your brand's reach. Minimizing your risk.

P2P Marketplaces enable brands to recapture the secondary and tertiary sales of their own products, while tracking provenance, and also to extend their branding by facilitating the sale of complementary products with no inventory or operational risk.

We recognize that a marketplace is both operationally intensive for the seller and technically challenging for a brand to implement internally. We work through those pain points with you to create a well managed, profitable, and liquid marketplace that represents your brand accurately. Working with your brands and sellers needs every step of the way.
